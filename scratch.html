Favorite Activities

  1. Swimming at the beach
    • We stayed an an AirBNB across the street from the beach
    • We saw SO many turtles! I even got some videos up close
  2. The Road to Hana (specifically the waterfall we swam at)
    • It's a giant winding road you can drive and take hikes on! It can take all day
    • My favorite part was finding this beautiful waterfall pool that you could swim in
    • This is where we ate fresh fruit and experienced so many cool things
  3. Snorkeling
    • My first time going! Went to a couple places and it was so cool!
    • Sometimes when the waves were rough it was hard, but it was a blast
  4. North Shore Ziplining
    • The place was located on WW|| grounds
    • Surprisingly wasn't scary and the workers were so kind and fun
  5. Boat ride snorkeling tour
    • You take a tour on a boat where they take you out in the ocean to snorkel instead of near the shore!
    • It was a cool experience, but I definitely got motion sick...
    • We saw so many cool fish!
    • Was worth going once but I can't say I'd do it again
