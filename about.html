About Me

Let's get to know each other!

My name is Haley Ann! I grew up in Orem/Lehi Utah, and I started BYU in fall of 2021. I graduated from Lehi High School in 2021, and I served a mission for my church in Reno, Nevada speaking Spanish from 2022-2023.

My whole life, my mom has owned some kind of business—from making bows to decorating cookies—and I grew up watching her chase her dreams. It wasn’t until high school that I realized I was a lot like her, and that I had dreams of my own to follow. That’s when I knew I wanted to study business in college. Since then, I’ve stuck with it and have enjoyed every moment.

Growing up, I was always surrounded by art—from painting to theater, creating has always been a passion of mine. Whether through internships or a small business I started in college, I’ve always been driven by the creativity of others and the desire to support it. Over the years, I’ve built both skills and experience, and I’m excited about the opportunity to keep growing and learning.
